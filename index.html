Flora is a seed repository dedicated to collecting(1), archiving(2) and distributing(3) native Irish wildflowers. Over 100 native Irish plants are now classified as endangered. Flora is essential in preventing disease, climate change, and biodiversity loss. We rely on the support of people to assist in the re-introduction of these wildlflowers across Ireland by distributing seeds in their local area.
